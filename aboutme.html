About Me!!

Description

My name is Cody Skoglund and like I had mentioned before I have Cystic Fibrosis. Never in my life have I let this disease run my life. For the most part I have lived a normal life. I have a girlfriend, I have a really nice, job and I have some amazing friends who have stuck by my side since day 1. I am a huge fan of sports and love watching them in person. I would like to think I am funny and hard working no matter what my family says. I live with my mom, dad, and brother and they are my biggest supporters and they are the reason that I am writing this right now

Fun Facts

  • I am Currently a sophmore at Asnuntuck Community College
